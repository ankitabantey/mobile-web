At WEHack, we're looking to shape the future of technology

UTD's female-driven tech organizations are excited to join forces and present our first all female, non-binary, 24-hour hackathon. Our goal is to create an inclusive environment that highlights and empowers women and non-binary students through technology. We aim to encourage more underrepresented people to harness their skills to create projects of their own.

Theme
Robotics

In order to encompass both aspects of hardware and software, the theme of WEHack 2020 will revolve around robotics. We will provide hardware and software workshops so that students can learn and explore the basics of both in order to apply them to their project


Track
Software

Robots are geared by programs written by developers. These instructions enable them to complete complex tasks, from movement to speech.

Track
Hardware

Robots wouldn't be robots without their physical hardware. With their 'bodies', they are able to complete tasks set by software developers.

Venue: Erik Jonsson School of Engineering West



Frequently Asked Questions


Who can attend

People who identify as female or non-binary may sign-up to participate for this hackathon.If you do not identify as female or non-binary but you consider yourself an ally of our cause, we would love to have you as a mentor or volunteer! More info on how to become a mentor/volunteer will come out soon.

When and where is WEHack

WEHack 2020 will be happening from April 4th-5th and will be located in the ECSW building at the UTD campus!

Do I need to be a CS major/minor

No! WEHack 2020 is open to all majors and would be the perfect place to start your first coding project!

How do I apply?

Hacker and Mentor Applications are not open yet. Please check back soon for updates.

Can I participate if I am a high schooler?

If you are a high schooler who will be at least 18 years old by WEHack 2020, we would love to have you participate in our hackathon! If you will not be 18 by WEHack 2020, unfortunately you can not participate in our hackathon but feel free to stop by, take a look at some of our hackers amazing projects, attend workshops, and network with other students/companies!

Does this cost money?

WEHack 2020 is completely free! We will cover your overnight stay, meals (with plenty of snacks of course), swag, provided by our wonderful sponsors, and prizes!
